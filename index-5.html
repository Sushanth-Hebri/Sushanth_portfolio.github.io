Heat Beat  

(Web music player)



The Web Music Player with Visualizer is a project that I developed to provide music lovers with an interactive way to enjoy their favorite songs. The player includes a wide range of features that make listening to music a more immersive experience, such as a visualizer, playlist, and customizable settings.




Features:

  • Visualizer: The visualizer is the highlight of the project, allowing users to see a graphical representation of the music they are listening to.

  • Playlist: The player includes a playlist feature, allowing users to create and save playlists of their favorite songs. Users can also add or remove songs from their playlists at any time.

  • Customizable Settings: The player includes a range of customizable settings that allow users to adjust the playback, visualization, and audio settings to their preferences.





Technologies Used:

  • HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for front-end development

  • Visualizer by codepen




Conclusion:

The Web Music Player with Visualizer project was an exciting opportunity for me to combine my passion for music with my skills in web development.
